How To Play

This is The Simon Game. It tests your ability to remember patterns and helps improving it.

  1. When you'll click on start, a button will blink. You need to remember the button and then click it.
  2. If the button you clicked was the one that blinked earlier, the game will proceed and you'll enter the next level.
  3. If you click the wrong colored button, the game will end.
  4. Each time you press the correct button, a new colored button is added to the pattern.
  5. You need to remember the correct order and click the buttons according to that.
  6. Play the video below for some more help.
  8. Good Luck with the game!
